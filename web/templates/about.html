About

Data on riders and teams is from ProCyclingStats with teams from 1963 until 2016. Riders form the nodes of a graph, and two nodes are connected if the riders every rode on a team together (during the same season). The Floydâ€“Washall algorithm was used to calculate a shortest path between all pairs of riders. Note that there might be many such shortest paths, while only one of them is displayed.

The maximal distance to Eddy Merckx is 6, and eight riders are at this distance:

  • Mario Vonhof
  • Stefan Parinussa
  • Patrick Kohler
  • Steve Cunningham
  • Sven Biermann
  • Jordens Roman
  • Armin Kroniger
  • Serge Herz
The maximal distance between any pair of riders is 7, and 535 rider pairs are at this distance from eachother.
